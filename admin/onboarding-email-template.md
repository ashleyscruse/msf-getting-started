Welcome to the Morehouse Supercomputing Facility (MSF)! You've been added to our HPC allocation at TACC.

Follow the link in that email to create your account. Do NOT go to the TACC portal separately — use the link in the invitation email so your account is automatically connected to our project.

Once your account is created, follow the Getting Started guide here to set up MFA, log in, and run your first job:

[LINK TO YOUR GITHUB PAGES GETTING STARTED GUIDE]

A few important notes before you start:
- Do NOT use SMS/text messages for multi-factor authentication. Use an authenticator app (we recommend Okta Verify).
- Do NOT run compute-heavy work on the login node. Submit jobs through Slurm.
- If you're unsure which system to use, ask me before logging in.

If you run into any issues, feel free to reach out.
